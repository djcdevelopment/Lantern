# Product Boundaries

## Hearth (Existing GAD Website)

Already built.

Do NOT redesign from scratch.

Hearth is:
- the guild chronicle,
- retrospectives,
- raids,
- goats,
- charter,
- join flow,
- officer stewardship,
- memory and continuity.

Hearth should remain:
- quiet,
- sparse,
- archival,
- and chronicle-oriented.

The design effort should NOT focus here.

Only incremental refinement later if substrate-backed visualizations become valuable.

---

## GAD App / Workbench

This is the primary design target.

Workbench is:
- local-first,
- player-centric,
- inspectable,
- replay-capable,
- AI-assisted,
- and contribution-aware.

Workbench should feel like:
- a personal workshop,
- a raid review companion,
- and a local exploration tool.

NOT:
- an esports dashboard,
- a metrics wall,
- or enterprise analytics software.

---

## Existing Advanced Replay Surface

Already exists.

Preserve:
- replay rendering
- movement smoothing
- lerping
- encounter playback
- raid replay visualization
- local LLM integration

The replay surface should become:
- an advanced/team-analysis mode,
not:
- the first thing every player sees.

---

## Core UX Shift

Old mental model:
"Open app → giant raid replay view"

New mental model:
"Open app → what happened to ME last night?"

Progression should naturally expand:
me
→ encounter
→ team
→ replay
→ deeper analysis
